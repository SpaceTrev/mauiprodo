Our Mission


Sustainable Solutions:

We create Sustainable Solutions on Maui collaborating with our community and businesses. Using modern Information technology design and production, we are creating a Sustainability Platform providing information, networking and education. We are creating green jobs and green practices to launch Sustainability infrastructure, products, services and systems.

Plastic Free 2020 Campaign

Maui no ka oi. Maui is the best. This is why we are dedicated to increasing awareness, education and action to be plastic free. The fact is : plastic is a problem for our environment. Businesses list their sustainable practices that give anybody the ability to go plastic free, with simple yet effective practices such as encouraging reusable food and & beverage containers, refilling water and having recycling receptacle(s).

  • Digital Map listing subscribed local businesses with sustainable products,services & practices.
